About Me

Professional Background

Sifat Al Saad is a dynamic communication expert and humanitarian professional from Bangladesh, widely recognized for his leadership in youth development, social advocacy, and community-based initiatives. With a strong academic background and hands-on field experience, he has built a reputation for impactful communication, strategic outreach, and people-centered development work.

He is currently pursuing a Master's degree in Peace, Conflict, and Human Rights Studies at the Bangladesh University of Professionals (BUP), where his academic focus aligns with global peacebuilding, governance, and human rights advocacy.

Driven by a strong passion for international service, Sifat aspires to work with United Nations agencies and aims to become a United Nations Volunteer (UNV). His long-term vision is to contribute to sustainable development, peacebuilding, and humanitarian diplomacy at the global level.
